Memory Game

Welcome to the Memory Game!
Your objective is to find and match pairs of cards by flipping them over.
By playing regularly, you can enhance your memory, concentration, and pattern recognition skills.

This game is the basis of my thesis at the NYU Tisch School of Arts and is based on my mother's experience
It is a prototype in development and continuously being updated.
